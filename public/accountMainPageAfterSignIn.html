Analysis

Step 1: Go to https://forms.google.com/
Step 2: If not already logged in, log into your Google account.
Step 3: Click on the Blank template under the "Start a new form" section.
Step 4: Add a title to your form.
Step 5: Add questions to your form. Be aware you can change what type of question it is such as: Multiple choice, Checkboxes, Dropdown, etc.
Step 6: After you are all done adding all the questions you want click on the Send button in the top right.
Step 7: Click on the link icon and copy the link to the survey form and put that link into the Google Form link field.

Google Form Analysis Tutorial

Step 1: Go to https://forms.google.com/
Step 2: If not already logged in, log into your Google account.
Step 3: Click on the survey form that you want to analyze in the "Recent forms" section.
Step 4: Click on "Responses".
Step 5: Use the "Summary" tab to see various auto-generated graphs and statistics generated from ALL of the survey responses.
Step 6: Use the "Question" tab to see each question and its responses.
Step 7: Use the "Individual" tab to see each individual survey filled out.
